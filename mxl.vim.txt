Installation:
-------------
1. Copy csc.vim to: ~/.vim/syntax/mxl.vim or c:\program files\vim\vimfiles\syntax\mxl.vim
2. Add the following three lines of commands to $VIMHOME\filetype.vim
   augroup filetypedetect
   au BufNewFile,BufRead *.mxl			setf mxl
   augroup END
